Since I was a young coder I found it fun to teach others, especially if they where passionate to learn.

While at school, I was introduced to coding by my young cousin that sadly passed away far too early. But his kind heart and patience, sparked a love for coding and creating, that lives on through me.

It reminds me that in the same way my love for code was kindled and flamed on by his enthusiasm, so I can possibly help a few others that are keen on learning something new.

My aim is not to teach those that already know what they are doing, but rather to break down the simple concepts that is often overlooked. To foster a love for code and create a strong foundation to build from.
